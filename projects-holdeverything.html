The goal of this project was to design and develop a website for my client, Hold Everything of Florida, Inc. I chose a minimal design with refined aesthetics, and I believe the final product well suits their company.

Goals & Requirements

1

Image Gallery

The client's main request was for an image gallery to showcase their portfolio of work. Separating images into service categories was also important for the gallery feature.

2

Professional User Interface

The client did not have any direct specifications when it came to the UI for the website. My goal here was to create a design that was professional and complemented their area of business. Also, with a generally older audience, creating a minimal and straightforward design was important for the site's usability.
