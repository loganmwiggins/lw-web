InduServ Online

The goal of this project was to create a clean and professional company website for my client, InduServ LLC. InduServ offers cleaning services for businesses and residences, so I aimed for a simple and clean design to complement their work.

Goals & Requirements

1

Images

The client requested a section in the website that could be used to display images from customer jobs and more. I would accomplish this through a banner that rotates between different images.

2

Services

InduServ offers a large variety of cleaning services. It was important to the client that the site had a section listing each type of service that they provide to customers.

3

Contact Form

Another specific request that the client made was for a section in the website with a contact form in which users could submit inquiries and requests for pricing estimates. Users would be able to fill out the contact form on the site, and submissions would automatically be sent to my client's email.
