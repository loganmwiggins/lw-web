Project Details

GBA Fall '23 Shirt Design

For this project, I was commissioned to create two t-shirt designs for an FSU business fraternity. At the end of the day, they would select one t-shirt to represent their group within the fraternity and use my design to print shirts for their members.

Designs

I started by meeting with the client to get a feel for what they were looking to put on the shirts. After communicating with the client, I learned that they wanted two mock-ups for shirts that were a play on the cigarette brands, Camel and Malboro. Using Photoshop, I was able to produce front and back designs for two different shirt concepts.

Mock-ups

While creating the designs, the client told me that he would show the designs to the group's members to have them vote to choose one of the designs. Because of this I chose to create mock-ups for the client to make it easier for them to visualize the final product.

Final Product

Finally, the group voted on the Malboro-inspired design, and they sent it off to a T-shirt manufacturer to print the shirts. Below is a physical copy of the shirt with my final design.
